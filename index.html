Directions

  • LIE (East/West)

    Exit 53 (Sagtikos Pkwy), go south to Exit S3 (Pine Aire Drive), make left at light. Go 1/2 mile, follow bend in road to 1st stop sign. Make right (Saxwood Street). Club is on the right about 100ft down.

  • From Northern State (East/West)

    to Sagtikos Pkwy South (Exit 44), go to Exit S3 (Pine Aire Drive), make left at light. Go 1/2 mile, follow bend in road to 1st stop sign. Make right (Saxwood Street). Club is on the right about 100ft down.

  • From Southern State (East/West)

    to Sagtikos Pkwy North (Exit 44), go to Exit S3 (Pine Aire Drive), make left at light. Go 1/2 mile, follow bend in road to 1st stop sign. Make right (Saxwood Street). Club is on the right about 100ft down.

  • From Commack Road

    to Gran Blvd.
    *if coming from Expwy (travelling south) -> make left
    *if coming towards (travelling north) -> make right make left on Industry Court, go to Saxwood Street, make right, Club is on the left.
